## STEP 4: Edit Together (CAPCUT)
**Go to:** https://capcut.com (free, desktop or web)

### Timeline Assembly:

```
0:00 - 0:05  |  Black screen → Title "NUDELAUFLAUF! Das Musical" (gold text)
0:05 - 0:10  |  CLIP 7: Boy on hillside (spoken intro plays)

0:55 - 1:05  |  CLIP 5: Mountain aerial zoom-out (Chorus continues)
1:05 - 1:20  |  CLIP 3: Cheese shot + CLIP 2: Stirring (Verse 2)

1:40 - 1:55  |  CLIP 7: Sunset hillside (Bridge — emotional)
1:55 - 2:15  |  CLIP 4: Into the oven + CLIP 6: Golden reveal (Final Chorus)
2:15 - 2:25  |  CLIP 8: Victory pose on mountain (climax)
2:25 - 2:35  |  CLIP 6: Close-up casserole (outro)
2:35 - 2:40  |  Black screen, text: "...bitte nicht verbrennen." then "Perfekt."
2:40 - 2:45  |  Credits: "A Thomas Whiting Production"
```

### CapCut Tips:
- Use "Beat Sync" to auto-cut clips to the music
- Add text overlays for the German lyrics (yellow/gold, Playfair Display font)
- Add English subtitles below in smaller white italic text
- Use "Lens Blur" transitions between scenes
- Add slight zoom (Ken Burns effect) to any static images
- Use "Glow" effect on the cheese/oven shots
- Export at 1080p or 4K
